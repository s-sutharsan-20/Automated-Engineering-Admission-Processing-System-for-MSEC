How the Admission Process Works

  1. Submission of Application:
    • Step 1: Fill out the application form with your personal details, academic information, and your top three course preferences.
    • Step 2: After completing the form, submit your application by clicking the "Calculate Cutoff and Submit" button.
  2. Cutoff Calculation:
    • Step 3: Once you submit the application, our system automatically calculates your cutoff percentage. The cutoff is based on your 12th-grade marks in key subjects (Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics).
    • Step 4: The calculated cutoff will determine your eligibility for the courses you've selected.
  3. Course Allocation:
    • Step 5: The system reviews your cutoff and matches it against the eligibility criteria for the three courses youâ€™ve chosen.
    • Step 6: Based on your cutoff, you will be allocated one of your preferred courses, starting with your highest preference. If your cutoff meets the requirement for the first choice, it will be allocated. If not, the system checks your second and third choices. If none of your choices meets our cuttoff requirement no course will be allocated.
    • Step 7: If your course preference could not be allocated due to a lower cutoff, you may be provided with alternative options or guidance on what to do next.

Important Notes:

  • Make sure all information is accurate to avoid delays or errors in course allocation.
  • The course allocation is subject to seat availability and your cutoff score.
  • Contact our admissions office if you have any questions or need further assistance.
